Aircraft Information

SriLankan Airlines operates a modern fleet of Airbus aircraft including A320, A321, A330-200 and A330-300 models. Our aircraft are equipped with the latest amenities to ensure your comfort throughout the journey.

Baggage Information

Economy Class passengers are allowed one piece of checked baggage up to 30kg. Business Class passengers can check in two pieces up to 40kg. All passengers are allowed one carry-on bag up to 7kg.
